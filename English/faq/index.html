FAQ:

  • Where can I find Iron / Gold / Mese / Diamonds? Iron is found from y -130, Gold from y -300, Mese from y -500, and diamonds only from -1000. But you can also buy Iron, Diamonds, and Mese in the basement of the Adminshop.
  • How do I get money into my account? By putting money in the yellow ATM at the spawn, or taking a job and sell some things. See "Jobs" and "Economy" for more information.
  • How do I get a job? job acquire <job> Read more in "Jobs".
  • Can I become an admin? No. The server team goes to players themselves. Not the other way around.
  • How could I to protect my things? This is a very important topic, see best in "Protect property".
  • Why I cant craft any hoe? -> Read the "Jobs" Article for more information.
