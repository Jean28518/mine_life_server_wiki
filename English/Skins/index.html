  • Skins

In Minetest you can have your own skin too. However, a skin depends on the server, and must be allowed by the server. If you want to set up your own skin for your player character on Mine Life, this is for a donation of your choice (from 1 € or 1$) to https://www.paypal.me/LinuxGuides (Yes, I'm having a YouTube channel as well) possible. Then write LinuxGuides privately in the Discord. Your player name, your PayPal name, the donated amount (for security), and your skin should be included. The skin will be assigned to your player account on the server as soon as possible, and after the next server restart you will be able to see your account with your new skin :)

I don't want to make money with this server, but I want to cover my monthly server costs at least a little bit :)

PS: Of course the "old" skins from Minecraft are supported ;)
