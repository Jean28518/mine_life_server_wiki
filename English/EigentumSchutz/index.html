  • Protecting your Property

To make sure your items and your property are safe, you should be aware of the following:

  • At the beginning, you should make sure that your entire property is protected. You can see that at the bottom left of the screen. If there is no entry, then you should do so urgently. Take a look at the "Areas" article.
  • Even if your property is protected, your chests are not automatically protected! Only in a "protected chest" are your items safe. For this you need a normal chest, and an steel ingot. If you do not have any steel ingot, you can/ should buy it for example from the adminshop :)
  • Furnaces and workbenches are unfortunately not protected!
  • Wooden doors can also be opened by others. Fix this problem with iron doors.
